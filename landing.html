Why We Built This

This project was born from a simple idea: to make the digital world a bridge to the physical one for those who cannot see it.

Our assistant uses advanced AI to help blind and visually impaired individuals navigate daily life with more confidence. From finding lost keys on a cluttered desk to reading a sign across the street, VisionLink AI provides the verbal descriptions needed to understand one's surroundings.
